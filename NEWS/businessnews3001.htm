1/25/01 SAN DIEGO--Got a knack for writing software? Whether developing software for a hobby or wanting to increases one's cash flow, opportunities exist to profit from innovative shareware products. However, knowing how to write code does not guarantee knowing where or how to market, distribute or sell the resulting product -- not to mention how difficult and time-consuming the process can be. Enter DigitalCandle with a solution to meet the needs of independent software developers.

"DigitalCandle is a company created by shareware developers for shareware developers. We understand what developers need to capitalize on their own products. We know the industry, and are aware of the pitfalls and traps that await developers," said Casey Vieceli, president and chief executive officer of DigitalCandle Inc. "DigitalCandle provides benefits that essentially give developers a 'leg-up' to succeed in the competitive software market."

DigitalCandle offers an effective, competitive approach towards generating sales and Increased revenue from independently developed software products. In exchange for services, DigitalCandle receives a percentage of generated product sales, but not until current monthly sales have been exceeded.
